Chef Cai's Mushroom Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 medium diced onion
  • 400 grams of sliced fresh mushrooms - shitake/portebello
  • 1 knob of butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cooking cream

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large soup pot. Saute mushrooms and add a pinch of salt until the mushrooms release their juices. Take about 10 minutes.
  2. Reduce heat to medium low and continue to cook, evaporating the liquid and caramelizing mushrooms (about 15 minutes).
  3. Add diced onions into pot and cook till onions are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes
  4. Stir in flour into the pot and cook, stirring often, for about 2 minutes
  5. Add water, chicken broth, thyme and garlic into the pot and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Puree soup with blender until smooth and thick. Taste and add more water as necessary. Stir in cream. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Soup is ready to be served
